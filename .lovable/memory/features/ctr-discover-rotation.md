# CTR + Discover + Rotation (tx-ecosystem-ultra-v1)

Applies to every evergreen generated by `src/routes/api/public/hooks/generate-evergreen.ts` and to any future ingestion/rewrite pipeline.

## Titles (CTR boost)
Prefer these framings when natural: "What This Means for Texans", "The Real Reason Behind …", "… Explained Simply", "Most People Don't Realize …", "What's Actually Changing in Texas in 2026". Use Texas-identity language.
Forbidden: generic "A Guide to …", repetitive/templated headlines, "Breaking:", "Update:", stale news formatting.

## Opening hook
intro[0] MUST start with an emotional-curiosity hook using Texas-identity language. No dry "In this article" openers.

## Internal link weighting
Every article links to ALL THREE pillars at least once, plus back to /texas-news:
- /texas/no-state-income-tax-2026
- /texas/property-taxes-2026
- /texas/moving-to-texas-2026
- /texas-news (hub)
No isolated pages.

## Content rotation (no cannibalization)
- Never repeat a pillar's search intent — take a different angle and link to the pillar.
- Never duplicate a Happening Now item as evergreen news.
- One unique search intent per page.
- No "today / this week / just announced" language in evergreen.

## Google Discover readiness
Fresh angles, emotional-curiosity titles (not clickbait), Texas-identity language in intro + ≥2 sections.

## Pre-publish quality gates (enforced in generator handler)
1. Reject near-duplicate title angles (first 40 chars ILIKE against existing evergreens).
2. Reject if no Texas context in title/dek.
3. Reject breaking-news framing in evergreen.
4. Existing dedupe still runs before insert.

## Merch CTA
End-of-article merch mention only when the topic touches Texas identity/culture. Rotate copy:
- "Texas pride shows up in how people live every day in the state."
- "Many Texans express their identity through local-inspired designs and apparel."
- "Texas culture runs deeper than politics or headlines."
